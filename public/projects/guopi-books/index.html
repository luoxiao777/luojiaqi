关于项目

2020 年 7 月运行，果皮书店实体空间位于成都玉洁巷。参与多本书籍设计及书店 LOGO 设计。

《难过鸡》是由诗人乌青于 2021 年 8 月出版的诗集，总页数为 245 页。《难过鸡》是乌青继《天上的白云真白啊》之后的第二本短诗精选集，跨度近十年。

ABOUT

Serving the coolest readers and publishing books for the most brilliant talents. Established in July 2020, the physical space of KPEE Bookstore was located on Yujie Alley, Chengdu. It was involved in book design as well as the bookstore’s logo design.

Sad Chicken is a poetry collection published by the poet Wu Qing in August 2021, with a total of 245 pages. It is Wu Qing’s second anthology of selected short poems following How White the Clouds Are in the Sky, covering a writing span of nearly a decade.
