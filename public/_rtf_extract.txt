1.ELIXIR31012  2025
关于：
ELIXIR31012于2023年在伦敦创立。本次项目主要负责LOGO设计更新以及延展应用和包装。两位来自英国皇家艺术学院的跨领域艺术家创立，由3D艺术家徐婧与声音艺术家夏己韫共同打造，两位设计师深耕时尚设计，将拟动画，声音艺术，可穿戴装置和表演等多媒体元素融合到品牌中，通过探讨后人类主义，神秘主义与地下俱乐部文化之间的关系。品牌核心理念在于探讨身体，意识与时空的边界，通过想像力与新媒体技术来突破这些界限，根植于青年亚文化群体，通过时尚配饰设计和新媒体艺术，传递出当下Z世代青年敢于冒险的创新精神。
ABOUT:
ELIXIR31012 IS A PIONEERING ACCESSORIESBRAND ESTABLISHED IN LONDON IN 2023, CO-FOUNDED BY 3D ARTIST XU JING AND SOUND ARTIST XIA YIYUN. IT EXPLORES THE RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN POSTHUMANISM, MYSTICISM AND UNDERGROUND CLUBCULTURE, USING ACCESSORIES AS ITS MEDIUM OF EXPRESSION.

3.CYPHER 2022
关于：
CYPHER理发店于2020年在西安成立。主要负责LOGO设计及延展应用。这里不仅仅是理发店，更是以社区为核心的交流中心、爱好交流的论坛、以及文化传承的空间。 CYPHER在这里具有文化、传递、凝聚力三种含义，在黑人文化中代表着社群之间的交流。字母C的运用意味着以文化接纳与交流为基本的开放包容，在这里不仅提供理发场所，还提供一种文化交流的场所，还可以让受众BARBERSHOP中体验到深刻归属。
ABOUT:
CYPHER BARBERSHOP WAS FOUNDED IN XI’AN IN 2022. IT IS MORE THAN JUST A BARBERSHOP; IT IS A COMMUNITY‑CENTERED HUB FOR CONNECTION, A FORUM FOR SHARING INTERESTS, AND A SPACE FOR CULTURAL INHERITANCE.
THE NAME “CYPHER” CARRIES THREE MEANINGS HERE: CULTURE, TRANSMISSION, AND COHESION. IN BLACK CULTURE, IT REPRESENTS COMMUNICATION AND CONNECTION BETWEEN COMMUNITY MEMBERS. THE USE OF THE LETTER “C” SYMBOLIZES OPENNESS AND INCLUSIVENESS, ROOTED IN CULTURAL ACCEPTANCE AND EXCHANGE.
THIS PLACE OFFERS NOT ONLY HAIRCUT SERVICES BUT ALSO A VENUE FOR CULTURAL INTERACTION, ALLOWING EVERYONE WHO COMES TO THE BARBERSHOP TO EXPERIENCE A STRONG SENSE OF BELONGING.

10.春来和
关于：春来和是一家陕西本地的老字号酒楼，以融合菜为主。设计通过提出“家是什么”的概念将物品和感受连接回忆，“聚在一起的饭桌、用习惯了的餐具、夹在筷子里的爱、春日的一场家庭聚会、儿时的宴席、开动前的期待、提起杯子的祝福”等场景与家联系在一起，激起观者回想时的具象化场景。碗作为宴请中基本的盛放食物的工具，对于中国人来讲是饮食习惯、礼仪文化和精神的物质载体；对于个人而言，成套的碗出现在宴席等场景提供人们使用，是家的另一代名词，承载记忆的同时象征着家人之间团圆美满，也蕴含着家庭成员之间其乐融融、和气和谐。
品牌设计及策划。
ABOUT：
CHUN LAI HE IS A TIME-HONORED RESTAURANT NATIVE TO SHAANXI, SPECIALIZING IN FUSION CUISINE. THE DESIGN LINKS OBJECTS AND FEELINGS TO MEMORIES THROUGH THE CONCEPT OF "WHAT IS HOME", CONNECTING SCENES SUCH AS "A DINING TABLE WHERE PEOPLE GATHER, FAMILIAR TABLEWARE, LOVE HELD BETWEEN CHOPSTICKS, A FAMILY GATHERING IN SPRING, BANQUETS FROM CHILDHOOD, ANTICIPATION BEFORE A MEAL, AND TOASTS WITH BLESSINGS" TO THE IDEA OF HOME, EVOKING VIVID, TANGIBLE SCENES IN THE VIEWER’S RECOLLECTION.
AS A BASIC VESSEL FOR SERVING FOOD AT BANQUETS, A BOWL IS A MATERIAL CARRIER OF DIETARY HABITS, ETIQUETTE CULTURE AND SPIRIT FOR THE CHINESE PEOPLE. FOR INDIVIDUALS, COMPLETE SETS OF BOWLS PROVIDED FOR USE ON OCCASIONS LIKE BANQUETS ARE ANOTHER SYNONYM FOR HOME. THEY CARRY MEMORIES, SYMBOLIZE FAMILY REUNION AND PERFECTION, AND EMBODY THE HARMONY, WARMTH AND CONGENIALITY AMONG FAMILY MEMBERS.
