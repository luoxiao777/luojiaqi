ELIXIR31012 is a London‑based accessories brand founded in 2023.
For this project I was responsible for refreshing the logo and developing a visual system that could extend across packaging and other applications.

The brand is co‑founded by 3D artist Xu Jing and sound artist Xia Yiyun. Their practice sits between fashion design, digital animation, sound art, wearable objects and performance, and the visual identity reflects this hybrid, experimental character.

The design explores the relationship between post‑humanism, mysticism and underground club culture. It focuses on the body, consciousness and the perception of time and space, using new media tools and imaginative visuals to express the energy of a young sub‑cultural community.
