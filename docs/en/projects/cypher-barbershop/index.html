CYPHER Barbershop is a community‑focused barbershop in Xi’an. It is not only a place for haircuts, but also a hub for conversation, shared interests and cultural exchange.

For this project I designed the logo and extended visual system for posters, signage and printed materials. The identity is built around the letter “C”, which stands for culture, circulation and cohesion, echoing the idea of the “cypher” circle in Black culture where people communicate and pass energy between each other.

ABOUT

CYPHER Barbershop was founded in Xi’an in 2022. It is more than just a barbershop; it is a community‑centered hub for connection, a forum for sharing interests, and a space for cultural inheritance.

The name “Cypher” carries three meanings here: culture, transmission, and cohesion. In Black culture, it represents communication and connection between community members. The use of the letter “C” symbolizes openness and inclusiveness, rooted in cultural acceptance and exchange.
