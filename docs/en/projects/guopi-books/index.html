ZH EN

KPEE BOOKS is an independent bookstore and publishing project in Chengdu. Since its opening in July 2020 on Yujie Alley, the space has combined book retail, small‑scale publishing and public events.

I worked on multiple book covers and interior layouts, as well as the bookstore’s logo and visual identity.

One of the key projects is Sad Chicken, a poetry collection by Wu Qing published in August 2021 (245 pages). It is his second anthology of short poems after How White the Clouds Are in the Sky, spanning nearly a decade of writing.

ABOUT

Serving the coolest readers and publishing books for the most brilliant talents. Established in July 2020, the physical space of KPEE Bookstore was located on Yujie Alley, Chengdu. It was involved in book design as well as the bookstore’s logo design.

Sad Chicken is a poetry collection published by the poet Wu Qing in August 2021, with a total of 245 pages. It is Wu Qing’s second anthology of selected short poems following How White the Clouds Are in the Sky, covering a writing span of nearly a decade.
