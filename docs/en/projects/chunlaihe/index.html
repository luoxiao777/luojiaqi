Chun Lai He is a long‑established restaurant in Shaanxi, specialising in fusion cuisine.

The visual identity is built around the question “What is home?” — connecting everyday objects and situations (the dining table, familiar tableware, family gatherings, childhood banquets, the anticipation before a meal, the gesture of raising a glass) with the emotional memory of home.

The bowl, as a basic vessel for serving food, becomes a key symbol. It carries not only eating habits and etiquette, but also collective memory and the idea of reunion and completeness for Chinese families.
