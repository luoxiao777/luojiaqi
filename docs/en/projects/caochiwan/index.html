Caochiwan is a historic village on the southern slope of the Qinling Mountains — a model site for rural revitalization and the first “nature education village” in the region.

For more than ten years, 42 crested ibises have lived in harmony with 52 local households here. The village is quiet and secluded; the birds bring a sense of spirituality and poetry, while contemporary life injects energy and movement.

I worked on the park brochure, event posters, packaging and other visual materials, building a system that connects the imagery of the ibises with the everyday life of the village.

ABOUT

Caochiwan is an ancient village located at the southern foot of the Qinling Mountains. It serves as a model village for rural revitalization in the Qinling region, China and the first village for nature education in the Qinling Mountains, China.
