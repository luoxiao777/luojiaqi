ELEVATOR is an underground club space in Shanghai, located on an upper floor of a narrow building in the former French Concession.  
It is a place for dancing, performance, exploration and conversation.

For this project I created poster graphics for different nights and events, translating the club’s energy and spatial feeling into print.

## ABOUT

On the fourth floor of a narrow building with a special elevator in Shanghai's former French concession dances elevator.
